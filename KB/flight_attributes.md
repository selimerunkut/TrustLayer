# 3. Germany (EU) Flight Rules

## Applicable Regulation
EU Regulation 261/2004

### Applies To
- Flights departing from any EU airport (any airline)
- Flights arriving in EU on an EU airline

Examples:
- Lufthansa
- Air France
- KLM

---

## Delay Compensation

### Arrival Delay ≥ 3 Hours

| Distance | Delay | Compensation |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| ≤ 1,500 km | 3+ hours | €250 |
| 1,500–3,500 km | 3+ hours | €400 |
| > 3,500 km | 3–4 hours | €300 |
| > 3,500 km | 4+ hours | €600 |

### Key Rules
- Delay measured at arrival time
- Minimum delay threshold is generally 3 hours
- Compensation independent of ticket price

---

## Passenger Assistance

### 2+ Hour Delay
Airline must provide:
- Meals/snacks
- Refreshments
- 2 phone calls
- 2 emails/faxes

### Overnight Delay
Airline must provide:
- Hotel accommodation
- Transport between hotel and airport

### 5+ Hour Delay
Passenger may:
- Cancel trip
- Receive full refund

---

## Cancellation Compensation

### Cancellation Notice ≥ 14 Days
- No compensation

### Cancellation Notice 7–13 Days

No compensation if:
- Replacement departs ≤2 hours earlier
- Replacement arrives ≤4 hours later

Otherwise:
- €250–€600 compensation

### Cancellation Notice < 7 Days

No compensation if:
- Replacement arrives ≤1 hour from original arrival time

Otherwise:
- €250–€600 compensation

---

## Extraordinary Circumstances

No compensation if disruption caused by:
- Extreme weather
- Political instability
- Air traffic control restrictions
- Security risks
- Third-party strikes

Passenger assistance still required.

---

## Germany-Specific Rules

- Compensation claims expire after 3 years
- Delay measured at final destination arrival

# 4. Colombia Flight Rules

## Applicable Regulation
RAC 3 (Aerocivil)

### Applies To
- Flights departing from Colombia
- Any airline nationality

---

## Delay Assistance & Compensation

| Delay Duration | Assistance | Compensation |
|---------------|------------|--------------|
| 1–3 hours | Snack + 3-minute phone call | None |
| 3–5 hours | Snack + meal + communication | None |
| 5+ hours | Meal + accommodation (if overnight and not home city) | 30% of affected flight segment |

---

## Compensation Rules

### Requirements
Compensation only applies if disruption was caused by airline fault:

Examples:
- Crew scheduling issues
- Maintenance problems
- Operational decisions

### Compensation Amount

30% of affected flight segment fare:
- Excludes taxes
- Excludes airport fees
- Paid in cash by default

Passenger may choose:
- Voucher
- Miles
- Cash

---

## Cancellation Rules

### Airline Fault Cancellation

Passenger receives:
- Refund of unused ticket portion OR
- Rebooking

Plus:
- Minimum 30% compensation on affected segment

---

## Extraordinary Circumstances

No compensation for:
- Weather
- Airport closures
- Air traffic control restrictions
- Security events
- Strikes outside airline control

# 5. Other South American Jurisdictions

## Brazil (ANAC)

### Delay Rights

4+ Hour Delay:
- Re-accommodation
- Full refund
- Alternative transport

### Assistance

| Delay | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| 1 hour | Communication |
| 2 hours | Food |
| 4 hours | Accommodation |

### Compensation

Only for denied boarding:

| Type | Compensation |
|--------|-------------|
| Domestic | 250 SDR |
| International | 500 SDR |

No mandatory EU-style delay compensation.

---

## Chile (Law 20.831)

### Compensation
- Exists for delays and cancellations
- Depends on route and disruption

### Domestic Cap
- Up to 250 UF per passenger

---

## Argentina

### Delay Rights

3+ Hour Delay:
- Ticket refund within 7 days

No EU-style fixed compensation framework.

---

# 6. Germany ↔ Colombia Route Examples

## Germany → Colombia

Example:
- Frankfurt → Bogotá

Characteristics:
- Long haul
- ~9,000 km+

Applicable Regulation:
- EU261

Compensation:

| Delay | Compensation |
|---------|--------------|
| 3–4 hours | €300 |
| 4+ hours | €600 |

Additional Rights:
- Assistance
- Refund option after 5 hours

---

## Colombia → Germany

Example:
- Bogotá → Frankfurt

Characteristics:
- Long haul
- ~9,000 km+

Applicable Regulation:
- Colombian RAC 3

Compensation:

| Condition | Compensation |
|------------|--------------|
| 5+ hours + airline fault | 30% of ticket segment |
| Weather / ATC / strikes | None |
